2. What we collect — and why

We only collect data necessary for the App to do its job: give you and your partner a private space to collect, plan, and remember shared experiences.

When you create your account:

  • Email address — so you can sign in and recover your account
  • Password (encrypted, never stored in plain text)
  • Your first name — shown to both of you in the App
  • Your partner's first name — entered during onboarding
  • Optional: your anniversary date — for anniversary reminders

While you use the App:

  • Wishes and bucket-list items — title, category, budget, date, notes, checklists
  • Memories — title, note, date, photo(s), mood emoji
  • Photos you upload to memories, items, or as your couple photo
  • Couple ID + pairing code — a generated 6-character code for the shared view
  • Local notification permission — purely local to your device, no server push

What we do NOT collect:

  • No tracking across other apps or websites
  • No ad trackers, identity-linked analytics, or fingerprinting
  • We don't sell data or share it with advertising networks
  • No access to your contacts, location, or microphone

3. Legal basis (GDPR Art. 6)

  • Contract performance (Art. 6(1)(b)) for everything necessary for the App to function.
  • Legitimate interest (Art. 6(1)(f)) for one-time generation of your pairing code.
  • Consent (Art. 6(1)(a)) for optional features like local notifications, photo access, or calendar export. Withdraw anytime via iOS Settings.

4. Payment and subscription data

Korby offers a monthly subscription, a yearly subscription (with a free trial), and a one-time lifetime purchase. The current prices are shown to you in the App Store before purchase. Purchase and billing run exclusively through your Apple App Store account; Apple is the seller (Merchant of Record). We receive no credit-card or payment data. To manage subscription entitlements we use RevenueCat, processing a pseudonymous app user ID, the subscription status (active/trial/expired), and the App Store account country. Legal basis: Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR.

5. Who can see your data?

  • You via your own App installation.
  • Your partner, once paired with your code. Couples are private.
  • Us as the operator, only as necessary for maintenance and debugging. We don't actively read your content.
  • Our technical service providers (see section 6) — without active access to your content.

7. Where is the data stored?

Data is processed via Supabase Inc. (DPA per Art. 28 GDPR). Server location: Frankfurt am Main, Germany (region eu-central-1). Photo files are stored in the same data center in a private bucket, accessible only to members of the respective couple (row-level security). Supabase sub-processors: https://supabase.com/legal/dpa

8. How long do we keep your data?

As long as you keep your account. You can delete it in full from the App's settings via "Delete account and all data". This deletes your auth record, all wishes, memories, notes, and photos (from database and storage), and the couple entry. Deletion is immediate and irreversible.
